Experience Effortless Journeys with Ambassador Transportation

Established in 2005, Ambassador Transportation Corp. stands as a locally owned luxury ground transportation service nestled in the scenic Comox Valley on Vancouver Island, BC. Our unwavering commitment to safety is the bedrock of our service, shared not just by the East family but by every member of our dedicated team. Embracing our motto, 'Ambassador transports you in safety and style,' we seamlessly blend safety with sophistication. Holding licenses from the British Columbia Passenger Transportation Board and the National Safety Certification Board, we exemplify compliance and safety standards, ensuring your journey is secure and reliable. Our fleet surpasses local, provincial, and federal regulations, prioritizing the quality, safety, and reliability of your travel experience. Rooted in a legacy built on safety, elegance, and steadfast dedication, we are driven by the belief that every mile should exceed expectations and embody excellence.
