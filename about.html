About Ambassador

Ambassador was founded on a vision of providing the best ground and airport transportation service possible by capitalizing on our extensive knowledge of Vancouver Island and demanding the best from our highly professional and experienced drivers. We are licensed with the British Columbia Passenger Transportation Board and the National Safety Certification Board. We are fully insured and in compliance with all provincial and federal regulations. Ambassador vehicles meet and exceed local, provincial and federal requirements. “Ambassador transports you in safety and style.” Kevin East Owner

History of Ambassador

Established in 2005, Ambassador Transportation Corp. is a locally owned and family operated luxury ground transportation service located in the Comox Valley, on Vancouver Island, BC. With a commitment to safety, the East family and all of the employees and drivers are positive and passionate in everything they do.
