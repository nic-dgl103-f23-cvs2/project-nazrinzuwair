Your Guide to Competitive Pricing and Services

Understanding Our Pricing Structure

Our pricing is designed to offer clarity and flexibility. Here's a breakdown of how our rates are structured:

  • Zone-Based Pricing: We categorize destinations into zones to streamline our pricing. Each zone corresponds to a fixed rate for the first passenger in a party. Additional passengers within the same party incur a nominal extra charge per person, ensuring fair and competitive pricing for groups traveling together.
  • Hourly Rates for Charters: For non-airport charters originating in the Comox Valley, we offer hourly rates for various vehicle options. Whether it's a 6 Passenger Van, 14 Passenger Van or Sprinter, or an 8 Passenger Stretch Limo, our transparent hourly rates come with minimum charges and additional time billed in convenient 15-minute increments.
  • Additional Charges: Please note that additional pickup charges may apply for multiple pickup locations, ensuring seamless service for all passengers. All our prices include 5% GST and are subject to change to reflect market conditions.

This structure allows us to cater to diverse travel needs while maintaining a transparent and straightforward pricing system. For more details or specific quotes, feel free to contact us directly.
