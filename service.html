Exploring the Richness of Wine Tourism: Unveiling Exquisite Experiences Amidst Vineyards

Embarking on a wine tour is more than just a journey through vineyards—it's an immersive exploration of culture, taste, and craftsmanship. Our wine tour service encapsulates the essence of this exquisite experience, inviting enthusiasts and novices alike to delve into the world of winemaking amidst picturesque landscapes. Each tour we offer is crafted to indulge the senses, providing a behind-the-scenes look into the artistry, dedication, and flavors that shape each bottle of fine wine. From the serene ambiance of vineyards to the stories shared by passionate vintners, our wine tours promise moments of discovery and delight, elevating your appreciation for the heritage and allure encapsulated in every sip.

Wine Time

This fun and educational wine tour provides you with behind the scenes access to the Comox Valley’s award winning wineries. Taste the locally made wine, meet the wine makers, and learn how your favourite local wines are produce! Packages from $125 per person. Tour group must be 6 guests.

Brews Cruise

This fun and educational brewery tour provides you with behind the scenes access to the Comox Valley’s craft breweries/brew pubs. Taste the locally crafted beer, meet the brewery staff, learn how your favourite beers are made and enjoy an appetizer at each stop! Packages from $125 per person. Min. 6 people per tour.

Sip & Savour Tour

A Comox Valley culinary adventure includes appies and your choice of wine or craft beer at 3 different restaurants in one night.Locations are kept secret to maximize fun and excitement. Let our guides show you the best our Valley has to offer! Packages from $125 per person. 6-8 people per tour.
